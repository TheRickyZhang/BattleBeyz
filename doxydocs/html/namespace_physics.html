◆ accumulateFriction()

void Physics::accumulateFriction ( BeybladeBody * beyblade,
StadiumBody * stadium )

Simulates the effects of beybldae-stadium friction contact. May be unintuitive if you are not familiar with Beyblades.

Applies a positive linear accelaration tangential to movement at driver-stadium contact. Applies a negative angular accelaration to oppose the beyblade's rotation.

The magnitude of accelaration is given by the sum of a traditional friction model and a rotation-speed dependent model. The relative strengths of these can be altered as parameters in Physics.

Parameters
beyblade[in] Pointer to the beyblade body.
stadium[in] Pointer to the stadium body.
